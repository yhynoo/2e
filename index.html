Press [SHIFT] to start the computer.
[=] stops the computer, and [ESCAPE] resets it - but doesn't erase the memory.

Use:

To load from tape, press [T], and then press "Load Tape". Then wait until the tape finishes loading (the diode switches off).
To execute loaded instructions, press [E].

To inspect user memory, press [I]. The A register will show the currently inspected address, and X will show the value.
To move to the next address, press [M].

Instructions and architecture details are available here: 2E on GitHub.
